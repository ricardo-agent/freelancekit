The free freelance admin stack: a tool for every task

A practical guide — 6 min read

The work you get paid for is the fun part. The admin around it — invoices, rate math, contracts, proposals, follow-ups, chasing new clients — is the tax you pay to be your own boss. You don't need expensive software to handle it. Here's a genuinely free tool for each part of the grind, plus the honest free alternatives, and the fastest way to do all of it from one place.

Invoicing

🧾 A clean, client-ready invoice

Fill in the work, get an invoice you can print or save as PDF — no account, nothing uploaded: the free invoice generator.

Also free: some accounting apps have a free invoicing tier; a spreadsheet template works in a pinch but you'll rebuild the math every time.

Pricing

🧮 The hourly rate that actually funds your life

Most freelancers set their rate by feel and undercharge. The free rate calculator does the income-costs-tax-hours math and gives you the floor.

Also free: a spreadsheet, if you know the formula — the calculator just saves you building it and getting the billable-hours part wrong.

Contracts

📋 Catch the traps before you sign

Paste any contract or SOW into the free red-flag checker and it flags the risky clauses — IP on signature, Net-60, unlimited revisions — in plain English.

Also free: a lawyer friend, or reading every clause yourself. For anything high-stakes, still get a real lawyer.

Proposals

📝 A proposal built to win

Turn a brief into a client-ready proposal — outcome-framed, with anchored pricing — using the free proposal generator.

Also free: a docs template you fill in by hand each time (and re-format, and re-price…).
